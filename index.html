“Collagen creams can penetrate the dermis layers”

Noting that collagen molecules are fairly large, it's almost close to unlikely that collagen creams when applied topically on the surface of the skin will penetrate deep into the dermis layers. While partially hydrolyzed collagen or transdermal technologies are available, the effectiveness of that permeability remains largely on the surface.
